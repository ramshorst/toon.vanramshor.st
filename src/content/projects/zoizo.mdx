## Context

Zoizo (zoiseau.net) is a French company behind a series of six small books designed to help parents support the psychomotricity of their newborns. The product is educational and intimate — the books come with an accompanying digital experience through QR codes that unlock videos tied to each title.

## What I did

I worked alongside a graphic designer who created the illustrations, implementing the design in Shopify and defining the UI style to ensure consistency across the full storefront. The work covered product pages, the overall shop experience, and the branded details that make a small-run e-commerce feel considered — including transactional email templates.

## Gated digital experience

Each book includes a QR code giving buyers access to a private video section tied to their purchase. The implementation required a custom access flow within Shopify — linking a physical product to a protected digital experience — which extended the scope beyond a standard storefront build.
