## Context

Trafik is a well-regarded graphic design studio in Lyon with a strong focus on cultural clients: theatres, operas, schools, and cultural institutions. The studio is known for rigorous, typographically-led work and a serious approach to visual communication.

## What I did

I worked across design and development, contributing to projects for cultural institutions and exhibition contexts. The work spanned visual identity, print, editorial, and exhibition materials — the kind of work where every detail of composition, hierarchy, and typography is considered carefully.

## Why it mattered

More than any technical output, this period was formative for my graphic eye. Being immersed in a studio working at that level of craft — where the visual language of institutions is shaped with precision — gave me a reference point for quality in design that I still carry today. It connected my architectural training in spatial and compositional thinking to the discipline of graphic communication.
