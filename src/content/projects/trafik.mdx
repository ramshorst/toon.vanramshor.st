## Context

Trafik is a well-regarded graphic design studio in Lyon with a strong focus on cultural clients: theatres, operas, schools, and cultural institutions. The studio is known for rigorous, typographically-led work and a serious approach to visual communication.

## What I did

I worked on front-end development and 3D work for art pieces. On the exhibition side, I was involved in design and production — producing the plans, technical details, and coordination needed to bring physical installations to life.

One project was a neon light installation for a Lacroix Sports exhibition: large suspended geometric forms made of real neon tubes, requiring precise production drawings and close work with fabricators to get the structure, proportions, and lighting right.

## Why it mattered

This period sharpened both sides of my practice. The 3D and front-end work kept me close to the technical, while the exhibition production work — where a design decision becomes a physical object in a space — connected my architectural background to real fabrication constraints. Being inside a studio working at that level of craft gave me a reference point for quality I still carry today.
